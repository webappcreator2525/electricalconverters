Contact Us

Found an error, want a new calculator, or have a question? We'd like to hear from you.

ElectricalConverters is maintained by an electrical engineering student and a small editorial team. We read every message and especially want to know about any calculation that looks wrong — accuracy is the whole point of this site.

Email

Reach us at contact@electricalconverters.com. We aim to reply within a few business days.

What to include

  • Reporting an error? Tell us the page URL, the values you entered, the result you got, and the result you expected.
  • Suggesting a calculator? Describe the conversion and a typical use case.
  • General question? Include enough detail (voltage, phase, circuit type) so we can help precisely.

Before you write

For safety-critical decisions such as breaker or wire sizing, please consult a licensed electrician and your local electrical code — we can explain a formula, but we cannot sign off on an installation. See our About page for how we check our tools.
