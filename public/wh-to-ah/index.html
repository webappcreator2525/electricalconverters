Formula: Ah = Wh ÷ V

How to convert Wh to Ah

The formula is Ah = Wh / V.

For example, 1200 Wh at 12V = 100 Ah; the same 1200 Wh at 24V = 50 Ah. This shows why voltage is strictly required when determining capacity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the Ah change with voltage?

Amp-hours measure charge, while watt-hours measure total energy. To get the same total energy at a higher voltage, you need fewer amp-hours.

Can I convert without knowing voltage?

No, the conversion is meaningless without system voltage.

Is this used for lithium batteries?

Yes, but always use the nominal voltage (e.g. 12.8V for LiFePO4) for the most accurate calculation.
