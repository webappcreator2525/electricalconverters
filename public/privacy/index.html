Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At ElectricalConverters ("we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is extremely important to us. Because our tools are designed purely as engineering reference calculators, we operate on a principle of absolute data minimization.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal data. ElectricalConverters does not use accounts, does not ask for your email address, and does not track individual user identities. All of the calculation tools on this website are executed 100% locally on your device (client-side) using JavaScript.

Any numbers, voltages, or specifications you input into our calculators never leave your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Cookies

We do not set or read any cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes. Your browsing session remains strictly your own.

3. Server Logs and Hosting

Our website is hosted using Cloudflare. As a standard practice for internet infrastructure providers, Cloudflare may temporarily log anonymous routing data (such as IP addresses, browser types, and timestamp requests) strictly for security, DDoS protection, and network routing purposes. This data is not used to identify individual visitors.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, any changes will be posted directly on this page with an updated revision date.

5. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to return to the homepage and use the available tools, as no data is collected!
